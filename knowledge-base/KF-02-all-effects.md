## Interface Notes

1. **Sub-tab toggle**: Top of Effects panel shows "Video effects" (left) and "Body effects" (right)
2. **Search**: Search field at top — "Search for effects"
3. **Favorites/Collected**: Star icon at top for saved effects
4. **Grid layout**: 6 effects per row with thumbnail previews and small text labels
5. **Download arrows**: Most effects show download arrows (need to download before first use)
6. **PRO indicators**: Blue/purple diamond badge on premium effects — scattered throughout, not category-wide
7. **Effect preview**: Click thumbnail to preview on active clip in canvas
8. **Effect application**: Drag to timeline or double-click to apply
9. **Right panel properties** (when applied): Effect name, file path, size, transform (position X/Y), opacity, duration, keyframe controls, "Stay in original location" toggle
