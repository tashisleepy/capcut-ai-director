## Interface Notes

1. **Access**: Left Panel > Transitions tab (split-arrow icon)
2. **Application**: Drag between two adjacent clips on timeline, or double-click
3. **Duration**: Adjustable by edge-dragging on timeline
4. **Grid layout**: 6 transitions per row with thumbnail previews
5. **Search**: "Search for transitions" field at top
6. **Favorites**: Star icon at top for saved transitions
7. **Download arrows**: Most transitions need download before first use
8. **PRO indicators**: Blue/teal diamond badge — ~90% of transitions are PRO
9. **Right panel properties** (when selected): Name, Duration, Stay in original location toggle, Transition in/out toggles, Alignment, Codec info
