## NOTES

- **Featured, NEW, and Hits** are curated/algorithmic categories — most filters in these also appear in their specific category (Life, Portrait, Retro, etc.)
- **PRO concentration:** Retro and Alternative categories have the most PRO-locked filters, especially in the second half of each list
- **Film stock emulations:** Found in Retro category — Agfa 400, Gold 200, Kodak 500T, Provia 100, VHS I/II/III
- **Movie-inspired filters:** Named after actual films — Wong Kar-wai, Oppenheimer, Barbie, Dune 1/2, Inception, Deadpool, Black Panther, Poor Things, Don't Look Up, GTA I/II/III
- **Era-specific retro:** 1968, 1978, 1980, 1998, 80s Holiday, 80s Hongkong, 90s 2, Y2K Vogue
- **Pet category** is dedicated with pet-themed names (Paw-radise, Furry Clarity, Cat-eye Green, Ma-cat-rons, etc.)
